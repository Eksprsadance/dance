    About Us

    Eksprsa Dance is a leading dance school in Long Island City, teaching a diverse variety of ballroom and Latin dance, including American style and International style, both social and competitive levels. We work with beginners and advanced dancers of all ages, and offer an adult and children`s programs, as well as customized wedding dance and intensive fitness programs. Our lessons are taught by the internationally acclaimed dance instructors, including active competitive professional dancers, award-winning performers and major TV show participants.

    Services
    PRIVATE DANCE LESSONS

    The most effective way to learn how to dance. During your private lesson you will receive personalized instruction focused on your needs. Our private lesson is 45 minutes long, for one or two people to attend.

    GROUP CLASSES

    A great way to learn different dance styles, pick up fun moves, meet new friends and get in shape in a fun group atmosphere. You will have an opportunity to dance with various partners throughout the class. The group class is 55 minutes long. Classes are for beginners through advanced levels.

    SPECIAL OCCASION DANCE LESSONS

    Dancing is fun and social, and builds poise and confidence. Out instructors will help you to look and feel great for any special event – anything from wedding to a black tie gala, even if you have never danced before.

    CHILDRENS BALLROOM

    Ballroom dancing for children is a great way to develop am important skills as discipline and focus, grace and esthetics and to improve social and communication skills. Addition to fun, children can compete in an effort to build artistic and performing skills. Private and group classes are available.

    EKSPRSA INTENSIVE FITNESS

    A signature cardio workout group class with elements of Latin and Modern dance styles. This class is sure to burn calories and put a smile on your face. The class is 55 minutes long.

    SOCIAL PRACTICE PARTY

    We play all ballroom and latin music including: Salsa, Merenge, Swing, Hustle, Tango, Waltz, Foxtrot, Cha-Cha- Cha and more. The best way to practice and celebrate what you learn. Stay tuned for news about our next party.
