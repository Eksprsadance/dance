Overview

Eksprsa Dance is located in New York City and offers a diverse variety of ballroom and Latin dances at both the social and competitive level from beginner to advanced. Further, we instruct dancers of all ages, and offer an adult and children’s programs, as well as customized wedding dances and intensive fitness programs. Our lessons are taught by internationally acclaimed dance instructors, including active competitive professional dancers, award-winning performers and major TV show participants. The mission behind Eksprsa Dance is to provide a solution to the vast and growing demand for sophisticated and advanced ballroom dance instruction in NYC – the world’s epicenter for art, culture and dance. Every day, more men, women and children in New York City are seeking to either learn and/or improve their dance. Indeed, it has been demonstrated scientifically that dancing keeps you young, as it is good for the brain, posture, hand-eye coordination, not to mention the intangible benefits of introducing art and fun into one’s life.

Where to Start

If you are a complete beginner, or an experienced performer, we recommend that you try our new client special offer, which includes two private introductory lessons. During these lessons, you will learn the basics of various dances, focusing on the dances which interest you most. The instructor will work with you to develop a tailored program that fits you and your goals. Alternatively, we also offer group classes for children and adults. Please see our calendar or call us to find the right classes.

Services

PRIVATE DANCE LESSONS

The most effective way to learn how to dance. During your private lesson you will receive personalized instruction focused on your needs. Our private lesson is 45 minutes long, for one or two people to attend.

GROUP CLASSES
See Our Schedule

A great way to learn different dance styles, pick up fun moves, meet new friends and get in shape in a fun group atmosphere. You will have an opportunity to dance with various partners throughout the class. The group class is 55 minutes long. Classes are for beginners through advanced levels.

SPECIAL OCCASION DANCE LESSONS

Dancing is fun and social, and builds poise and confidence. Out instructors will help you to look and feel great for any special event - anything from wedding to a black tie gala, even if you have never danced before.

CHILDRENS BALLROOM
See Our Schedule

Ballroom dancing for children is a great way to develop am important skills as discipline and focus, grace and esthetics and to improve social and communication skills. Addition to fun, children can compete in an effort to build artistic and performing skills. Private and group classes are available.

EKSPRSA INTENSIVE FITNESS
See Our Schedule

A signature cardio workout group class with elements of Latin and Modern dance styles. This class is sure to burn calories and put a smile on your face. The class is 55 minutes long.

SOCIAL PRACTICE PARTY

We play all ballroom and latin music including: Salsa, Merenge, Swing, Hustle, Tango, Waltz, Foxtrot, Cha-Cha-Cha and more. The best way to practice and celebrate what you learn. Stay tuned for news about our next party.
