About Us

Eksprsa Dance is a leading dance school in Long Island City, teaching a diverse variety of ballroom and Latin dance, including American style and International style, both social and competitive levels. We work with beginners and advanced dancers of all ages, and offer an adult and children`s programs, as well as customized wedding dance and intensive fitness programs. Our lessons are taught by the internationally acclaimed dance instructors, including active competitive professional dancers, award-winning performers and major TV show participants.
